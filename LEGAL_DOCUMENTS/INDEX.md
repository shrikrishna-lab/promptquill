- **Key Rights:** Access, correction, erasure, portability, restriction, objection
- **Contact:** dpo@promptquill.dev

### ✅ CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- **Applies to:** California residents
- **Documents:** Privacy Policy
- **Key Rights:** Know, delete, opt-out, non-discrimination
- **Contact:** privacy@promptquill.dev
- **Sections:** Privacy Policy Section 10.1

### ✅ COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- **Applies to:** Users under 13
- **Documents:** Privacy Policy, Terms of Service
- **Requirements:** Parental consent, minimal data collection
- **Sections:** Privacy Policy Section 11; Terms Section 3

- **Applies to:** All users receiving marketing emails
- **Features:** Opt-out links, business address, immediate processing
- **Reference:** Privacy Policy Section 1; Acceptable Use Policy

### ✅ GDPR Data Processing (Article 28)
- **Legal Basis:** Data Processing Agreement

- **Safeguards:** Encryption, access controls, audit rights
