## 5. REFUND POLICY

### 5.1 Non-Refundable Items

**The following items are NOT refundable:**
- Purchased credits (used or unused)
- Subscription days (if you cancel mid-cycle)
- Content generated using credits
- Access to features during subscription
- Bonus credits from promotions or referrals

**Why:** Credits have immediate value upon purchase

### 5.2 Refundable Circumstances

Refunds are issued ONLY for:

**1. Duplicate Charges**
- You were charged twice for the same purchase
- Example: Charged $9.99 twice for one subscription
- Refund: Full duplicate charge amount

**2. Unauthorized Transactions**
- Transaction made without your consent
- Example: Account compromised, fraudulent charge
- Refund: Full transaction amount
- Timeline: Within 30 days of charge

**3. Billing Errors**
- You were charged incorrect amount
- Example: Charged $19.99 instead of $9.99
- Refund: Difference in amounts

**4. Service Unavailability**
- Service completely unavailable > 24 consecutive hours
- Example: Outage prevented all feature access
- Refund: Pro-rated credit for downtime
- Must be reported within 7 days

**5. Card Declined/Failed Payment**
- If payment declined, card not charged
- If retried without consent, refundable
- Contact support for resolution

### 5.3 Refund Process

**To Request a Refund:**

1. **Contact Support:**
   - Email: support@promptquill.dev
   - Subject: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"

2. **Provide Information:**
   - Account email
   - Transaction date
   - Amount charged
   - Reason for refund
   - Screenshots or evidence (if applicable)

3. **Our Review:**
   - We review within 5-10 business days
   - We assess if refund is warranted
   - We contact you with determination

4. **Refund Issued:**
   - If approved: Refunded to original payment method
   - Processing time: 5-10 business days to appear in account
   - Notification sent confirming refund

### 5.4 Refund Timeline

**Refund Status:**
- Requested → Under Review (5 days)
- Approved → Processing (5 days)
- Complete → Funds in account

From request to account: 10-20 business days typical

### 5.5 Unsuccessful Refund Attempts

If refund fails:
- We reattempt up to 3 times
- If failed after 3 attempts, issue credit instead
- Credit applied to account as voucher
- Usable immediately for credits or subscription
