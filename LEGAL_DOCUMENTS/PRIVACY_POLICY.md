## 6. DATA RETENTION

### 6.1 Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| **Account Data** | Until account deletion | Service provision |
| **Payment Records** | 7 years | Legal/tax requirements |
| **Email Preferences** | Until opt-out | Service & legal compliance |
| **Login Logs** | 90 days | Security |
| **Support Tickets** | 2 years | Customer service & legal |
| **Consent Logs** | 2 years | GDPR compliance (then deleted) |
| **Failed Payment Attempts** | 1 year | Fraud prevention |
| **Usage Analytics** | 1 year | Service improvement |
| **Deleted User Audit Trail** | 5 years | Legal audit trail |

### 6.2 Deletion Process
Upon account deletion:
1. Account data marked as deleted immediately
2. Content archived for 90 days (deletion window)
3. After 90 days, all content permanently removed
4. Payment records kept for 7 years (legal requirement)
5. Audit trail retained for 5 years
6. Email address can be reused after 90 days

## 10. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

### 10.1 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident:
- **Right to Know:** Request what personal data we collect and use
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal data
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt out of "sales" of personal data (we don't sell)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** No discrimination for exercising rights
- **Right to Correct:** Correct inaccurate personal data

**Making a Request:**
- Email: privacy@promptquill.dev
- Response time: 30 days
- Verification: We verify your identity before processing

### 10.2 General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union:
- **Right to Access:** Download your personal data
- **Right to Correction:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Export data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing for marketing
- **Rights Related to Automated Decisions:** Challenge automated profiling

**Making a Request:**
- Email: dpo@promptquill.dev or privacy@promptquill.dev
- Include: Your ID, subject of request, details
- Response time: 30 days (45 days for complex requests)
- We verify your identity before processing

### 10.3 Other Rights

**Residents of other jurisdictions may have rights including:**
- Opt-out of profiling
- Opt-out of automated decision-making
- Right to know about automated processing
- Right to human review of automated decisions
