## 1. INTRODUCTION

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") outlines the standards of conduct for users of Prompt Quill's Service. By using the Service, you agree to comply with this Policy.

We reserve the right to enforce this Policy at our sole discretion, including removing content, suspending accounts, or terminating service.

## 2. PROHIBITED CONTENT AND ACTIVITIES

### 2.1 ILLEGAL CONTENT AND ACTIVITIES

Users are strictly prohibited from:

**Crime and Violence:**
- Creating content for illegal purposes
- Generating instructions for weapons, explosives, or illegal acts
- Creating content promoting violence or terrorism
- Engaging in extortion, fraud, or blackmail
- Creating content related to human trafficking or exploitation
- Content related to illegal drug manufacturing

**Harassment and Threats:**
- Threats of violence or physical harm
- Cyberstalking or harassment campaigns
- Doxxing (publishing private information)
- Blackmail or extortion attempts
- Coordinated harassment or targeted abuse

**Exploitation:**
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or child exploitation in any form
- Non-consensual intimate imagery ("deepfakes")
- Sexual content involving minors
- Content sexualizing or exploiting children
- Any form of child exploitation

### 2.2 INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY VIOLATIONS

Prohibited uses:
- Generating content that infringes copyrights
- Creating content with trademarked material without permission
- Generating plagiarized content
- Creating content that violates patents
- Content misappropriating trade secrets
- Generating content impersonating others' intellectual property

### 2.3 FRAUD AND DECEPTION

Users cannot:
- Create fraudulent or misleading content
- Generate false credentials or documents
- Create content for phishing or social engineering
- Generate fake reviews or testimonials
- Create misleading financial or health claims
- Impersonate individuals, organizations, or entities

### 2.4 HATE SPEECH AND DISCRIMINATION

Prohibited content:
- Content promoting hatred based on race, ethnicity, or national origin
- Antisemitic or anti-Muslim content
- Content promoting hatred based on religion
- Gender-based hate speech or misogyny
- LGBTQ+ hate speech or transphobic content
- Hate speech based on disability
- Slurs or dehumanizing language targeting protected groups
- Content advocating discrimination or violence

### 2.5 MISINFORMATION AND DISINFORMATION

Users cannot create:
- False health/medical claims that could cause harm
- COVID-19 vaccine misinformation
- Election fraud or voting misinformation
- Climate denial or scientific misinformation
- Deliberately false news or propaganda
- Misleading financial or investment advice
- Content designed to deceive or manipulate

### 2.6 ABUSE OF THE SERVICE

Prohibited activities:
- Using automated tools to exploit the Service
- Attempting to access other users' accounts
- Creating multiple accounts to bypass restrictions
- Circumventing rate limits or security measures
- Overloading the Service with requests
- Reverse engineering the Service
- Scraping, crawling, or automated collection
- Selling or trading accounts or credits

### 2.7 HARASSMENT, BULLYING, AND ABUSE

Users cannot:
- Use the Service to target, bully, or harass individuals
- Create content designed to shame or humiliate
- Engage in coordinated harassment campaigns
- Create sexual or romantic content about real individuals without consent
- Harass or abuse staff or other users
- Spam or flood the platform

### 2.8 PRIVACY VIOLATIONS

Prohibited activities:
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Creating content with others' personal data (addresses, phone numbers, SSNs)
- Generating content from hacked or leaked data
- Violating confidentiality agreements
- Misusing others' biometric data
- Capturing or sharing private communications without consent

### 2.9 ADULT AND SEXUAL CONTENT

Guidelines:
- Adult content must be clearly marked and age-verified
- Non-consensual sexual content is prohibited
- Sex trafficking or exploitation content is prohibited
- Sexual content involving anyone without capacity to consent is prohibited
- Bestiality or zoophilic content is prohibited
- Content exploiting or sexualizing minors in any way is prohibited

## 3. CONTENT MODERATION

### 3.1 Our Moderation Approach

**We may:**
- Monitor user-generated content for policy violations
- Remove violating content immediately
- Suspend accounts for policy violations
- Preserve content for law enforcement
- Report serious violations to authorities

**We reserve the right to:**
- Remove content without advance notice
- Terminate accounts for serious violations
- Pursue legal action for criminal violations
- Report violations to law enforcement

### 3.2 Appeals Process

If your content is removed or account suspended:

1. **Notification:** You receive notification of removal/suspension
2. **Reason:** We provide reason for the action (if safe to do so)
3. **Appeal:** You can appeal within 30 days
4. **Review:** We review your appeal with fresh eyes
5. **Decision:** We notify you of appeal decision within 10 business days
6. **Reinstatement:** If appeal approved, content restored or account reinstated

**To Appeal:**
Email: support@promptquill.dev
Include: Account info, content details, explanation

## 7. CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATIONS

### 7.1 Enforcement Actions

**For Minor Violations:**
- Warning/notification
- Content removal
- Temporary account restriction (24-48 hours)
- Loss of specific features temporarily

**For Serious Violations:**
- Temporary account suspension (7-30 days)
- Permanent account termination
- Credit/payment forfeiture
- Legal action and law enforcement reporting

**For Criminal Violations:**
- Immediate permanent termination
- Full cooperation with law enforcement
- Potential civil or criminal prosecution
- Evidence preservation for authorities

### 7.2 Escalating Enforcement

**First Violation:** Warning + content removal

**Second Violation:** 3-day account suspension

**Third Violation:** 7-day account suspension

**Fourth Violation:** 30-day account suspension + review period

**Fifth Violation:** Permanent termination

*Serious violations (CSAM, violence, harassment): Immediate permanent ban*

## 12. APPEAL AND GRIEVANCE PROCESS

### 12.1 Formal Appeals

If you believe your content was wrongly removed:

**Step 1:** Submit appeal to support@promptquill.dev
- Include: Content/account details
- Explain: Why removal was incorrect
- Timeline: Within 30 days of removal

**Step 2:** We review with independent reviewer
- Review decision within 10 business days
- Fresh perspective on the decision
- Consider new information you provide

**Step 3:** Notification of decision
- If approved: Content restored, account reinstated
- If denied: Final decision (one additional appeal possible)
